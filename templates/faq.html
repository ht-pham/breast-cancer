Frequently Asked Questions

What is this application for?

This application uses machine learning models to analyze mammogram data and assist in predicting the likelihood of breast cancer.

How accurate is the prediction?

The app uses advanced machine learning algorithms, including KNN, SVM, Decision Trees, and Neural Networks, achieving an accuracy of 90% in evaluations.

What kind of data does the app require?

The app requires numerical inputs for 10 features derived from mammogram scans. These inputs are essential for accurate predictions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, the app processes data locally during your session and does not store or share any personal or sensitive information.

Which machine learning models does this app use?

The app uses four models: K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), Support Vector Machine (SVM), Decision Tree, and Neural Networks. Results from all models are displayed for comparison.

Can this app replace a medical diagnosis?

No, this app is a tool designed to assist healthcare professionals and researchers. It should not be used as a substitute for a professional medical diagnosis.

Who can use this app?

This app is designed for educational and research purposes. Healthcare professionals may find it useful for initial analysis, but it is not a diagnostic tool.
